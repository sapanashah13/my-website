About ISKCON Lalitpur

ISKCON Lalitpur is dedicated to spreading Krishna consciousness through bhakti yoga, spiritual wisdom, devotional service, and community outreach.

Through daily worship, Bhagavad Gita teachings, festivals, and prasadam distribution, we aim to inspire peace, devotion, and a meaningful spiritual life.
